BSF Jets SMS Opt-In Compliance Statement

This document explains how BSF Jets collects and manages SMS opt-ins to ensure compliance with Twilio's messaging guidelines.

1. How Consumers Opt-In

Users opt in to receive SMS notifications through an online form available on our website:

  • Consumers visit our Opt-In Page.
  • They enter their **phone number** in a required input field.
  • They check a **required consent checkbox** stating: "I consent to receive SMS notifications from BSF Jets. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out."
  • They click **"Subscribe"** to complete the opt-in process.

2. Opt-In Confirmation Message

After opting in, users receive an **automated SMS confirmation**:

"Thank you for subscribing to BSF Jets flight notifications. Message & data rates may apply. Reply STOP to unsubscribe, HELP for assistance."

3. How Opt-In Consent is Stored

Each opt-in submission is logged in our secure system, including:

  • Phone number provided.
  • Timestamp of opt-in.
  • Checkbox confirmation stored in the database.

4. How Users Can Opt-Out

Users can opt out of receiving SMS messages at any time by replying **"STOP"** to any message. They will receive a confirmation:

"You have been unsubscribed from BSF Jets notifications. You will no longer receive messages."

5. Support & Help

Users can reply **"HELP"** to any message for assistance, which will return our support contact:

"For support, contact BSF Jets at support@bsfjets.com."

6. Compliance with Twilio Guidelines

BSF Jets follows Twilio's best practices by ensuring:

  • Explicit consent is obtained before sending messages.
  • Clear opt-out instructions are provided in every message.
  • User data is securely stored and only used for messaging purposes.

7. Contact Information

For compliance inquiries, contact support@bsfjets.com.
